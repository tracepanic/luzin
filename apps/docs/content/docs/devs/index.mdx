Luzin is an open-source learning management system (LMS) designed to empower educators and institutions
with a flexible, feature-rich platform for creating great learning experiences. Luzin is built on a modern
tech stack, ensuring scalability, reliability, and ease of use. Luzin is built for educators, parents and
learners.

### Why Choose Luzin

Luzin offers everything an LMS is supposed to have, and more.
